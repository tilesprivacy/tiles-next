What is Tiles?

A private, collaborative AI assistant that works for you. Powered by on-device models and AT Protocol, Tiles is built for people who want intelligence without renting their memory to centralized providers. Born from the User & Agents community, our mission is to empower people by designing and building software that provides agency, control, and choice in our digital lives.

What’s coming next

  • Web UI for browser access
  • Remote Links to reach your assistant across devices.
  • Mini Apps for sharing tools and workflows
  • Group Chats for collaborative conversations with people and AI together.
